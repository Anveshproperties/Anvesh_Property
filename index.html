Anvesh Properties - Documentation Guide

Required Documentation Guide

For Salaried Employees
  • Signed application form along with a photograph
  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport
  • Address Proof: Passport, Utility bills (Elec/Gas/Water), or Driving License
  • Age Proof: School/College Leaving Certificate or Birth Certificate
  • Income Proof: ITR forms, Form 16, last 6 months salary slips & bank statements
For Self-Employed / Professionals
  • Signed application with 2 photographs
  • ID & Residence Proof (PAN, Passport, Voter ID, Ration Card, or Bank Statement)
  • Income: Last 3 years ITRs (Saral, Computation, P&L, Balance Sheet)
  • Business Proof: Partnership Deed, Registration, MOA & AOA (for companies)
  • Bank Statement: Current & SB A/C for last 6 months (Updated)
  • Processing Fees Cheque
  • Copy of Passport, Visa, Service Contract, and Salary Slips (Attested)
  • GPA (General Power of Attorney) in prescribed format (Attested)
  • Bank Statements: Overseas (1 year) and NRI (6 months)
  • All salary documents must be submitted in English
Purchase of New Flat (Builders)
  • Agreement to sale with builder & Registration Receipts (2 copies)
  • Blueprint attested by Corporation - Flat marked in RED
  • Title Investigation Report for the last 12 years
  • Commencement Certificate, N.A. Permission, and ULC order
  • Payment Receipts made to builder
Purchase of Resale Flat
  • Agreement to sale with vendor & Registration Receipt
  • Chain of agreements (Vendor to Builder)
  • Registered Conveyance Deed (Builder to Society)
Construction / Extension
  • Sale Deed / Lease Deed and Registration
  • 7/12 Extract, Index II, and Demarcation certification
  • Detailed Estimate certified by Licensed Surveyor/Architect
MHADA / PCNTDA / MIDC / MHB Plots
  • Allotment letter & NOC from the body
  • Lease deed & Registration receipts
  • Sanctioned building plan & Commencement certificate
Loan Take-Over
  • Outstanding loan quotation & Repayment Track Record
  • List of Documents & NOC for take over from current bank
  • Copies of Property Agreement & Sanction Plan (Blue Print)
  • Agreement to sale with the builder & Registration Receipts
  • Blueprint (Attested) - Flat marked in RED
  • Payment receipts to builder (Bank clearance required for Cheques)
  • NOC from the builder & Builder Demand Letter
Important: The above-mentioned income documents are also required for the Co-applicant if their income is being considered for the loan.
